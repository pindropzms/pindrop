Request Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your PinDrop account and all associated personal data, please follow the steps below. This process applies to the app published under the developer name PinDrop on the Google Play Store.

Steps to Request Deletion

  1. Click the button below to open your email client:
  2. Send an email to pindropzm@outlook.com with the subject line:

Subject: PinDrop Account Deletion Request

In the body of the email, please include:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your app username (if different)
  • An optional reason for deletion
Email Privacy Team

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Profile information (name, email, phone number)
  • Booking history (service type, addresses, dates, times)
  • Push notification tokens and device identifiers
  • Analytics events tied to your account

Data Retention

After deletion of your personal data, we will retain anonymized logs and records for up to 12 months for security, fraud prevention, and compliance purposes. These logs cannot be used to identify you.

If you have any questions about this process, please contact us at pindropzm@outlook.com.
