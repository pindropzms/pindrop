How It Works

Explore our seamless process for delivering excellent laundry and carwash services.

Schedule a Pickup

  • Booking: Use our user-friendly website or app to choose your pickup day.
  • Pack your bags: Use your Clean Drop bags if you’re a returning customer.
  • New Users: A 13-gallon kitchen trash bag works as your first Clean Drop bag.
  • Drop off: Provide your address or leave your laundry in the designated locker.

Expert Cleaning Process

  • Detailed Attention: We inspect every item and check pockets for a flawless wash.
  • Customized Care: Choose scented or unscented, fabric softener, or color-safe bleach.
  • Thorough Cleaning: Your garments receive a deep, expert clean for a spotless finish.
  • Professional Finish: Precision folds with an option for ironing for a perfect presentation.

Prompt and Secure Delivery

  • Flexible Returns: Choose next-day or two-day returns (additional k150 fee for next-day).
  • First Order Perk: Receive your laundry in our signature Clean Drop bag.
  • Safe Delivery: We leave your laundry at your desired spot or at a designated locker.
  • Final Touch: Experience a hassle-free delivery every time.
