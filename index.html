PinDrop is a fully registered Zambian company offering professional mobile laundry, house cleaning and on-site car wash services throughout Lusaka, delivered right to your doorstep. How It Works

LAUNDRY SERVICE

  • Booking: Use our user-friendly website or app to choose your pickup day.
  • Pack your bags: Continue to use your Clean Drop bags if you’re a returning customer.
  • Welcome, New Users: A 13‑gallon kitchen trash bag is equivalent to our branded Clean Drop bag.
  • Drop off your laundry: Share your address and contact information or leave your laundry in the designated locker.
Expert Cleaning Process
  • Detailed Attention: We sort each piece with a keen eye and check pockets.
  • Customized Care: Scented or unscented, fabric softener or color-safe bleach.
  • Thorough Cleaning: Deep, expert wash for spotless results.
  • Professional Finish: Precision folds and optional ironing.
Prompt and Secure Delivery
  • Flexible Return: Next‑day or two‑day returns (K150 extra for next‑day).

  • Cleaned and Dropped: Your laundry delivered fresh and folded.
