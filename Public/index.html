How It Works

LAUNDRY SERVICE

Schedule a Pickup

  • Booking: Use our user-friendly website or app to choose your pickup day.
  • Pack your bags: Continue to use your Clean Drop bags if you’re a returning customer.
  • Welcome, New Users: A 13-gallon kitchen trash bag is equivalent to our branded Clean Drop bag. It’s simple and straightforward for your first drop!
  • Drop off your laundry: Share your address and contact information or leave your laundry in the designated locker labeled “pick-up”

    • Expert Cleaning Process

  • Detailed Attention: We sort each piece with a keen eye, and check your pockets to prep for the perfect wash.
  • Customized Care: Whether you prefer scented or unscented, need fabric softener, or require color-safe bleach — we’ve got you covered.
  • Thorough Cleaning: Your clothes get a deep, expert clean, emerging spotless and fresh.
  • Professional Finish: Precision folds that would make a paper airplane jealous. Your clothes come back ready to impress. You have the option to have them ironed or not depending on your requested service.

    • Prompt and Secure Delivery

  • Flexible Return Times: Choose between next-day or two-day returns for your fresh, perfectly folded clothes. An additional k150 fee applies for next-day service.
  • New to Clean Drop?: You’ll receive your pristine laundry in our signature Clean Drop bags with your first order.
  • Flexible Delivery: Not home? No sweat. We’ll leave your laundry safe and sound in your desired spot or reschedule delivery. If a designated locker is in your area, you’ll find your laundry in your locker. Delaying pickup from lockers will attract a penalty fee.
  • Cleaned and Dropped: Consider your laundry day, delivered.
