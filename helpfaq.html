Help & FAQs

Welcome to PinDrop Help & FAQs. Here you'll find answers to common questions and guides to help you make the most of our mobile laundry and carwash services.

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

In the App: Download PinDrop from the Play Store or App Store, tap "Sign Up," enter your email and password, then verify your email.

How do I log in?

In the App: Open PinDrop, tap "Log In," enter credentials, and submit. Tap "Forgot Password?" if needed.

Using the Website

How do I schedule a pickup online?

After logging in using the app or on the website at pindropzm.com, fill in the form online and click the "Schedule" button. In the app, select service type, date, time, and address, then confirm your booking.

How can I view or modify my orders?

To modify orders, contact us on the contact info on the contact page

Pricing & Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Bank Transfer and Mobile Money (MTN, Zamtel, Airtel). No cash payments are allowed. See payment page

Account & Privacy

How do I delete my account?

Follow instructions on our Account Deletion page to remove your data permanently.
